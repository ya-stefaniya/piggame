Pig Game rules


You need to collect 100 point on global score to win.

The dice result is added to a current score.

Keep rolling to get more points.

If you roll 1 on the dice, next player gets the turn and you lose points.

Click "HOLD" to add current points to the global score. Then the next player starts.
